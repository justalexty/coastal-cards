# Calendar System Guide

## Overview

The game starts on **March 1st** and tracks real calendar progression with:
- Days of the week
- Lunar phases (29.5 day cycle)
- Seasonal changes
- Witch holidays and modern celebrations

## Key Dates & Effects

### 🧧 Lunar New Year (Biggest Holiday!)
- **When**: Varies yearly (late Jan/early Feb)
- **Duration**: 3 days of celebration
- **Effects**:
  - +20% reading accuracy
  - +50% tips from clients
  - Special "new beginnings" clients
  - Might find lucky money ($20 red envelope)
  - Croneslist luck bonus

### 🌸 Spring Equinox (March 20)
- **Effects**: 
  - +25% energy regeneration
  - New clients more likely
  - Flower vendors appear

### ☀️ Summer Solstice (June 21)
- **Effects**:
  - Extended daylight (work later)
  - +20 max energy
  - Beach location unlocked

### 🍂 Autumn Equinox (September 22)
- **Effects**:
  - +10% tips (harvest gratitude)
  - Cozy atmosphere bonus
  - Pumpkin spice everything

### ❄️ Winter Solstice (December 21)
- **Effects**:
  - +20% introspection readings
  - Shorter work days
  - Candles create ambiance

## Lunar Phases & Tarot

### 🌑 New Moon
- **Energy**: New beginnings, intention setting
- **Best for**: Fresh start readings, shadow work
- **Client mood**: Hopeful, ready for change
- **Special**: Can set monthly intention for +5% related readings

### 🌕 Full Moon  
- **Energy**: Peak power, emotional intensity
- **Best for**: Major revelations, manifestation work
- **Client mood**: Emotional, seeking answers
- **Special**: +30% divination bonus BUT exhausting
- **Warning**: Werewolf clients may appear...

## Rent Cycle

- **Due**: 1st of every month ($700)
- **Warnings**: 7 days, 3 days, 1 day before
- **Late fees**: 
  - Days 1-3: +$25
  - Days 4-7: +$50
  - Days 8+: +$100

## Minor Witch Holidays

### Beltane (May 1)
- Love readings doubled
- Everyone wants relationship advice
- Flower crowns are trendy

### Samhain (October 31)  
- Veil between worlds is thin
- Death card gains power
- Clients ask about departed loved ones

### Other Observances
- International Witch Day (March 31)
- Black Cat Appreciation (October 27)
- Broom Blessing Day (May 15)

## Gameplay Tips

1. **Plan for Lunar New Year** - Save vacation days, stock up on supplies
2. **Full Moon exhaustion** - Don't overbook, charge higher prices
3. **New Moon intentions** - Set goals that match your current needs
4. **Seasonal locations** - Beach in summer, cozy indoor spots in winter
5. **Holiday clients** - Special clients appear on major holidays

## Calendar UI

The calendar widget shows:
- Current date with day of week
- Moon phase with emoji
- Days until rent
- Any holiday celebrations
- Mini monthly calendar view

## Time Management

- Most clients: 9 AM - 7 PM
- Late night clients on full moons
- Holidays may have special hours
- Some locations close in bad weather
- Energy depletes faster in winter

## Special Moon Events

### Blue Moon (Rare!)
- Second full moon in one month
- Massive power boost
- Premium brooms more likely on Croneslist
- Special client guaranteed

### Blood Moon (Eclipse)
- Transformation readings x2
- Shadow work supreme
- Exhausting but incredibly powerful

### Super Moon
- All moon effects x1.5
- Emotional tsunami warning
- Coastal areas affected by tides
